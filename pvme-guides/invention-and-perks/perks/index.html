    Introduction

    This channel covers the perks we suggest for your gear and how to obtain them.

    ​

    When you see symbols in a listed perk (+ and X):

    ​

    + represents an item with multiple gizmos slots:

    E.g., +

    ​

    X - represents where you have a choice of perks:

    E.g., 'Biting 4 X' you could choose between 9 different perks to go with depending on your preference

    These combinations are listed in the later sections of this channel

    ​

    Related Guides & Links
    #invention-basics is a must-read if you're new to Invention #perk-information describes every perk we recommend. RuneScape Wiki's Perk Calculator lets you test out component combinations and see your chance of different perks. Shattered Worlds is the fastest way to level up your augmented gear. Video guides: Base Tank / Evil Lucario Note: Use 8 1 (1/1.08 at 120 invention) for Enlightened 4, instead of 8 as mentioned in the video. Alternatively, level up your gear using a slayer mob in #rs3-slayer.
