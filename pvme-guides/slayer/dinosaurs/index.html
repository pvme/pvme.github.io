    Introduction

    There are 4 types of dinosaurs (slayer monster), all of which are found on Anachronia:

    Feral dinosaurs - 90

    Venomous dinosaurs - 105

    Brutish dinosaurs - 99

    Ripper dinosaurs - 114

    ​

    Dinosaurs are only assigned by Laniakea and CANNOT be placed in the player owned dungeon.

    ​

    All of them attack with

    This makes the best style to use against them , followed by

    should not be used for any dinosaur tasks.

    ​

    Desirable Drops

    All dinosaurs have the same drop rates for uniques and Totem pieces. It is thus recommended to kill Venomous Dinosaurs if your level is high enough as they have the highest KPH.

    ​

    Dinosaur tooth - 1/800

    Needed for T3 Slayer Lodge for Slayer Helmet stand

    Blowpipe pieces - 1/6,000 on-task, 1/20,000 off-task

    Totem of auras middle - 1/300

    Totem of Summoning middle - 1/300

    Totem of intimidation base - 1/300

    Dinosaur rib bone - 1/1,000

    Needed for T3 Player Lodge for Skillcape stand

    ​

    During the Roar of Osseous, blowpipe and totem pieces have a 10% increase in drop rate.

    ​
