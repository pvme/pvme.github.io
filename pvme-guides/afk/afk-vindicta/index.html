    Positioning yourself in melee distance of where Vindicta spawns is not necessary, but helps.

    Ranged AFK Method (~48-50 kph)

    Overview

    Mandatory AFK Requirements
    ⬥ The following ARE REQUIRED for this method to work:     • Cutting corners will result in failure     • A more extensive list can be found in #AFK Overview:
    Items
    ⬥ Essence of Finality ⬥ Cryptbloom boots and gloves ⬥ Splintering Arrows ⬥ Scripture of Wen ⬥ Armour with Absorbative 4 ⬥ Armour with Lucky 6 ⬥ Igneous Kal-Xil or Igneous Kal-Zuk ​
    Other
    ⬥ Animate Dead ⬥ Ripper Demon     • Scrolls can be used to speed up kills, set to an autofire rate of 10. ⬥ Vampyrism aura ⬥ Pray Deflect Magic + Anguish

    ⬥ For fastest kill speeds, a Bow of the Last Guardian should be used. A Seren Godbow or Noxious Bow are slightly slower substitutes.

    ​

    Necromancy AFK Method (50+ kph)

    Overview

    Mandatory AFK Requirements
    ⬥ The following ARE REQUIRED for this method to work:     • Cutting corners will result in failure     • A more extensive list can be found in #AFK Overview
    Items
    ⬥ Amulet of Souls or ⬥ 2 pieces of Cryptbloom armour + or ⬥ Igneous Kal-Mor or Igneous Kal-Zuk ⬥ Armour with Enhanced Devoted 4 ⬥ Scripture of Jas or Scripture of Wen
    Other
    ⬥ Animate Dead ⬥ Vampyrism aura highly recommended for long AFK ⬥ Pray Deflect Magic + Sorrow     • Ruination requires additional prayer restoration

    ⬥ It is assumed that you will use a Legendary Pet for this setup.
