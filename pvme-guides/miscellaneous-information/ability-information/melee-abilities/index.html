    Introduction

    Note: Use the Table of Contents present at the bottom of the channel, or in the pinned comments, to quickly navigate through this channel and find the information you are looking for.

    ​

    Some general notes:

    ⬥ The game runs on an internal tick system: 1t (tick) = 0.6s (seconds).

    ⬥ The minimum time between abilities is called the global cooldown: 1 gcd = 3t = 1.8s.

    ⬥ Auto attacks will only give adrenaline if they hit (splash = no adrenaline gain).

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Debuff spells like Vuln or Enfeeble or Smoke Cloud do not give adrenaline.

    ⬥ Some useful sheets and resources are given below.

    Melee

    This section covers auto attacks, basics, thresholds, ultimate abilities and special attacks for Melee.

    ​

    Autos

    Default Melee Auto
    ⬥ Cooldown: Based on Weapon Speed     • Fastest: 4t (2.4s)     • Fast: 5t (3.0s)     • Average: 6t (3.6s)

    ​

    Basics

    Chaos Roar
    ⬥ Cooldown: 100t (60s) ⬥ Unlocked using a Chaos Roar ability codex ⬥ Doubles the base damage of the next melee ability cast within 7.2s     • So, it doubles all hits of bleeds (e.g. ) and multi-hit attacks (e.g. )     • Channels have multiple casts, so only doubles the first cast (e.g hit 1)

    ​

    Dismember
    ⬥ Cooldown: 25t (15s) ⬥ It is a Bleed:     • Not affected by or     • Hits every 2t (1.2s), for a total of 5 times ⬥ Can be extended:     • Strength skillcape passive adds 3 hits     • Masterwork Spear of Annihilation adds 2 hits ⬥ It is affected by     • Max hit increases by 20% per rank

    ​

    Backhand
    ⬥ Cooldown: 25t (15s) ⬥ Shares cooldown with Kick ⬥ Stuns for 2t (1.2s) ⬥ It is affected by     • Increases Max by 40% per rank     • Increases Minimum by 8% per Rank     • No longer stuns or binds

    ​

    Kick
    ⬥ Cooldown: 25t (15s) ⬥ Shares cooldown with Backhand ⬥ Stuns for 2t (1.2s) ⬥ Causes Knockback:     • Pushes 1x1 targets back 1 tile

    ​

    Barge
    ⬥ Cooldown: 34t (20.4s) ⬥ It is a Mobility ability:     • Will move you to Melee Distance ⬥ It is affected by Mobile     • Reduces cooldown to 17t (10.2s)     • Removes adrenaline gain ⬥ Binds for 11t (6.6s) ⬥ It clears Binds

    ​

    Greater Barge
    ⬥ Cooldown: 34t (20.4s) ⬥ All normal things apply ⬥ It has additional effects:     • Every tick (0.6s) since the player stopped attacking their target, gains +7% to its max range and +5% to its min, capping at 10t (6s) for a total of 100% and 20%. Damage range go from 75%-95% to 125%-165%     • After 8t (4.8s) pass since you stopped attacking, when you , if you cast / / / / within 10t (6s), it will convert the first one done into a damage over time ⬥ For details on how to use it, use !gbarge

    ​

    Bladed Dive
    ⬥ Cooldown: 34t (20.4s) ⬥ It is a Mobility ability:     • If you use it on a tile, it will you to that tile     • If you use it on a target it will you some point at Melee Distance of the target (the movement is accounted 1t later than had you clicked a tile) ⬥ It is affected by Mobile     • Reduces cooldown to 17t (10.2s)     • Removes adrenaline gain ⬥ It is an AoE:     • Hits 3x3 around where you land if used on a target     • This becomes 5x5 with Halberd ⬥ If you use it on a target and it dies within 10t (6s), the cooldown ends

    ​

    Cleave
    ⬥ Cooldown: 12t (7.2s) ⬥ It is an AoE

    ​

    Decimate
    ⬥ Cooldown: 12t (7.2s) ⬥ It does more damage against Shielded targets:     • It goes from a 110%-130% to 140%-160% ability

    ​

    Sever
    ⬥ Cooldown: 9t (5.4s) ⬥ It reduces target's damage by 10% for 8t (4.8s)

    ​

    Fury
    ⬥ Cooldown: 25t (15s) ⬥ Next melee attack has +25% increased crit chance

    ​

    Greater Fury
    ⬥ Cooldown: 25t (15s) ⬥ Next non-bleed melee hit within 15s is guaranteed to crit     • Only affects first hit of a combo ability     • Is applied only to 1 target in AoE     • Bleeds do not consume buff

    ​

    Havoc
    ⬥ Cooldown: 17t (10.2s) ⬥ Affected by Gloves of Passage , details in #armour-and-weapons

    ​

    Smash
    ⬥ Cooldown: 17t (10.2s) ⬥ Affected by Gloves of Passage , details in #armour-and-weapons

    ​

    Slice
    ⬥ Cooldown: 5t (3s) ⬥ Damage boosted on stun/bound targets:     • Becomes a 133%-161% basic ⬥ Affected by :     • Gain ( Rank * 0.6) more adrenaline     • Damage range is now 0.8 * original damage range

    ​

    Punish
    ⬥ Cooldown: 40t (24s) ⬥ Damage boosted when target is <50% health     • Becomes a 187.5%-237.5% basic

    ​

    Thresholds

    Slaughter
    ⬥ Cooldown: 50t (30s) ⬥ It is a Bleed:     • Not affected by or     • Hits every 2t (1.2s), for a total of 5 times ⬥ Triples damage when walked ⬥ Masterwork Spear of Annihilation adds 2 hits

    ​

    Blood Tendrils
    ⬥ Cooldown: 75t (45s) ⬥ It is a Bleed:     • Not affected by or     • Hits every 2t (1.2s), for a total of 5 times ⬥ Masterwork Spear of Annihilation adds 2 hits

    ​

    Assault
    ⬥ Cooldown: 50t (30s) ⬥ Channelled ability

    ​

    Destroy
    ⬥ Cooldown: 34t (20.4s) ⬥ Shares cooldown with Hurricane ⬥ Stuns for 6t (3.6s) ⬥ Channelled ability

    ​

    Hurricane
    ⬥ Cooldown: 34t (20.4s) ⬥ Shares cooldown with Destroy ⬥ It is an AoE:     • 3x3 with player as centre     • 5x5 with player as centre with Halberd-type weapon

    ​

    Flurry
    ⬥ Cooldown: 34t (20.4s) ⬥ It is an AoE:     • 3x3 with player as centre ⬥ Channelled Ability

    ​

    Greater Flurry
    ⬥ Cooldown: 34t (20.4s) ⬥ All normal things apply ⬥ Hits reduce cooldown by 2t/hit (1.2s/hit) for up to an 8t (4.8s)

    ​

    Forceful Backhand
    ⬥ Cooldown: 25t (15s) ⬥ Shares cooldown with Stomp ⬥ Stuns for 6t (3.6s) ⬥ It is affected by     • Increases Max by 40% per rank     • Increases Minimum by 8% per rank     • No longer stuns or binds

    ​

    Stomp
    ⬥ Cooldown: 25t (15s) ⬥ Shares cooldown with Forceful Backhand ⬥ Stuns for 6t (3.6s) ⬥ It causes Knockback     • Pushes 1x1 targets back 1 tile

    ​

    Quake
    ⬥ Cooldown: 34t (20.4s) ⬥ Applies a defence and affinity debuff:     • +2 affinity     • Reduces level by 5% of base if level is >90% of base ⬥ It is an AoE:     • 3x3 with player as centre     • 5x5 with player as centre with Halberd-type weapon

    ​

    Special Attacks

    Ek-Zekkil
    ⬥ Cooldown: 100t (60s) ⬥ Adrenaline Cost: 50% (45% with ) ⬥ Does not benefit from the special attack accuracy buff ⬥ Non-bleed hit followed by a 6-hit bleed     • Each hit deals a multiplicative 5% increase in damage from the previous ⬥ Masterwork Spear of Annihilation adds 3 hits. Requires ⬥ , , , , , , and 's parasite bleed extend the bleed by 1 hit each, capping at 5 extensions

    ​

    Zaros Godsword
    ⬥ Cooldown: 100t (60s) ⬥ Adrenaline Cost: 50% (45% with ) ⬥ On cast buff ⬥ 7x7 area (enemy you cast spec on takes damage every 3t (1.8s) while in it) ⬥ Increases damage by 25% from t0→34t (35t/21s duration)     • Buff starts t1 for autos, t2 for everything ⬥ Does not incur the auto cooldown, but will still attempt to force an auto ⬥ Can take weapon off and retain spec effect

    ​

    Dragon Battleaxe
    ⬥ Adrenaline Cost: 100% (90% with ) ⬥ Drains 10% (rounded down) of your , , , and levels and gain a level boost of 10 + 1 level for every 4 drained from your other stats ⬥ Boosts damage by 1.2 (stacks with for a 1.25 * 1.2 = 1.5 boost), or 1.1 when using , making it a 2 * 1.1 = 2.2 boost ⬥ Multiplicative 10% reduction to hitchance debuff ⬥ These effects lasts 1 minute ⬥ Can take weapon off and retain spec effect

    Vesta Longsword
    ⬥ Adrenaline Cost: 25% (22.5% with )

    ​

    Granite Maul
    ⬥ Adrenaline Cost: 50% (45% with ) ⬥ Can be used during the GCD

    Dark Shard of Leng
    ⬥ Cooldown: 25t (15s) ⬥ Adrenaline Cost: 30% (27% with ) ⬥ It is an AoE ⬥ Consumes all stacks ⬥ For every stack consumed     • Adrenaline Cost is reduced by 12%     • Each hit increased by 18-22%

    ​

    Ultimates

    Berserk
    ⬥ Cooldown: 100t (60s) ⬥ It is an on cast buff ⬥ It increases damage by 100% from t0→t33 (34t/20.4s duration) ⬥ Wearing 3+ pieces of Vestments extends the duration from t0→t33 (34t/20.4s) to t0→t43 (44t/26.6s) ⬥ It increases damage taken by 50% ⬥ Does not incur the auto cooldown

    ​

    Meteor Strike
    ⬥ Cooldown: 100t (60s) ⬥ It is an AoE:     • 3x3 with player as centre     • 5x5 with player as centre with Halberd-type weapon ⬥ Provides a Critical Adrenaline buff:     • For the next 30s, critical hits give 8% adrenaline     • This buff is applied on release of the ability

    ​

    Pulverise
    ⬥ Cooldown: 100t (60s) ⬥ Reduces damage dealt by target by 25% ⬥ If target dies to you gain 50% adrenaline

    ​

    Overpower
    ⬥ Cooldown: 50t (30s) ⬥ Affected by the Igneous Kal-Ket and the Igneous Kal-Zuk :     • Adrenaline cost reduced to 60%     • The ability has two hits.

    ​

    Massacre
    ⬥ Cooldown: 100t (60s) ⬥ It is mostly a Bleed:     • It does 6 hits, 5 of which are a bleed which means:     • Not affected by or     • Hits 5 times total, once every 2t (1.2s) ⬥ Masterwork Spear of Annihilation adds 2 hits

    ​

    Frenzy
    ⬥ Cooldown: 100t (60s) ⬥ Channelled ability

    ​

    Balanced Strike
    ⬥ Cooldown: 200t (120s) ⬥ Compares player and targets current HP percentage; Higher of the two takes damage based on that difference, and lower is healed for half of that. For details read wiki
